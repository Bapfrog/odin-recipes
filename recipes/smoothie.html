Cherry Berry Smoothie

Description

This cherry berry smoothie is a delicious conduit for healthy calories.

You great thing about this recipe is that you can "hide" many good ingredients in here, and no one (not even kids!) will be the wiser!

Ingredients

  • Frozen cherries
  • Frozen berry mix
  • Whole fat vanilla yogurt
  • Milk
  • Banana
  • Chia seeds
  • Baby spinach

Steps

  1. We're going to use a Magic Bullet mini blender for this receipe, pouring all of the ingredients into the tall tumbler.
  2. Add 1 cup of frozen fruit, using equal parts cherries and berries (though you're encouraged to experiment with ratios to find the mix that you like best).
  3. Add 1/2 of a banana, 1/2 cup of milk, and 1/2 cup of yogurt.
  4. Top this off with a tablespoon of chia seeds and a handful of spinach, and screw on the lid.
  5. Mix for about 10 seconds, then check the consistency - if it seems too thick (like, you turn the tumbler upside-down and the contents don't move), add a bit more milk. Either way, give it a good shake and then mix for another 10 seconds.
  6. That's it! Pour it into a cup or drink it straight out of the tumbler. This recipe provides a wonder assortment of macronutrients . . . just beware of the dreaded brain freeze!
